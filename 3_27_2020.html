Truth: While N-95 masks and N-99 masks prevent Coronavirus particles from being inhaled, The best way to prevent illness is to avoid being exposed to this virus through social distancing and staying away from public areas and group gatherings. The best prevention methods include hand-washing for 20 seconds with soap and water and other proper hygienic methods.
