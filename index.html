Welcome to LTL

       The purpose of our website is to clear up misconceptions regarding the Coronavirus to prevent further spread of panic and unnecessary social issues in addition to communicating all perspectives to the public.

       The Novel Coronavirus, also known as COVID-19 and SARS-CoV-2, has impacted the globe greatly in the past few months. Within 2 months, there have been more than 590,000 confirmed cases and 27,359 deaths around the globe. Panic quickly spread across the world along with the virus. However, false information is causing unnecessary panic that is causing other negative social consequences.
